Hi! Im Meeliza Jean
Mingasca

I find joy in quiet walks, the rhythm of a good jog, and the soul-soothing beauty of new places. Music moves me—whether I’m strumming my guitar or soaking in the sounds of jazz, indie pop, and R&B in a cozy venue. I’m here to create, connect, and explore—always with intention and heart.

Engaging in my favorite hobbies brings me immense happiness and excitement;

It allows me to escape from daily stress and negativity. Engaging in creative activities like painting and writing helps me express my emotions and find inner peace. Exploring nature through hiking and outdoor adventures refreshes my mind and energizes my spirit. Listening to my favorite music lifts my mood and inspires me to stay positive. Reading inspiring books broadens my perspective and provides a sense of fulfillment. Playing sports and staying active release endorphins, making me feel more vibrant and alive. Spending quality time with loved ones creates joyful memories that boost my overall well-being. Practicing mindfulness and meditation helps me stay grounded and calm amidst life's chaos. Traveling to new places satisfies my curiosity and gives me a sense of adventure. Overall, these interests are vital for my happiness, helping me release stress and embrace positivity every day.
